These links provide direct access to the primary sources that form the basis of our understanding of 802.1X security. They range from official standards and RFCs that define the protocol, to academic works that dissect its weaknesses, to industry guides that offer practical advice. Together, they offer a thorough and authoritative view of the attack vectors in 802.1X.
